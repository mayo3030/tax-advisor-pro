ITIN & Immigrant
Tax Guide

دليل الضرائب الأمريكية للمهاجرين وحاملي ITIN

Your complete guide to understanding Individual Taxpayer Identification Numbers (ITIN), filing taxes as an immigrant, and navigating US tax requirements. Available in English and Arabic.

Chapter 1

What is an ITIN?

ما هي رقم التعريف الضريبي (ITIN)؟

An Individual Taxpayer Identification Number (ITIN) is a nine-digit tax identification number issued by the U.S. Internal Revenue Service (IRS). It's used by people who are not U.S. citizens or permanent residents (green card holders) but have a requirement to file a U.S. tax return or report other tax information to the IRS. رقم التعريف الضريبي هو رقم من ٩ أرقام تصدره دائرة الضرائب الأمريكية للأشخاص اللي ليس لهم رقم ضمان اجتماعي (SSN) لكنهم محتاجين يقدمو إقرار ضريبي أمريكي.

🆔 ITIN Format

An ITIN looks like a Social Security Number (SSN) but starts with 9. Example: 9XX-XX-XXXX. You can use it in place of an SSN for federal tax purposes only. It does NOT provide work authorization or residency status.

رقم ITIN يبدأ بـ ٩. مثال: 9XX-XX-XXXX. تقدر تستخدمه بدل SSN للضرائب الفيدرالية بس. ما يعطيك تصريح شغل ولا حالة هجرة.

Key Facts About ITIN

✓

Valid for federal tax purposes only — Cannot be used for employment or Social Security benefits.

Issued by IRS — Not by Social Security Administration.

No work authorization — Does not grant legal status or permission to work.

Required to file taxes — If you earn income and must file a return but have no SSN.

Portable — Can be used immediately after approval (Form W-7).

صالح للضرائب الفيدرالية بس — ما تقدر تستخدمه بالشغل. الـ IRS هي اللي تصدره، ما تصريح شغل، بتحتاجه للضرائب، وتقدر تستخدمه فوراً بعد الموافقة.

Who Issues ITIN?

Only the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) issues ITINs. You cannot get an ITIN from your bank, employer, or state. You must apply directly to the IRS using Form W-7 (Application for IRS Individual Taxpayer Identification Number).

الـ IRS وحدها تصدر ITIN. ما تقدر تحصل عليها من البنك أو الشغل. لازم تقدم طلب مباشرة للـ IRS بفورم W-7.

💡
Important Note

Having an ITIN does NOT make you a U.S. tax resident for immigration purposes. Your immigration status is separate from your tax status. An ITIN is purely for tax filing.

وجود ITIN ما يعني إنك مقيم ضريبي أمريكي. حالة الهجرة منفصلة عن الضرائب. ITIN للضرائب فقط.

Chapter 2

Who Needs an ITIN?

من يحتاج رقم ITIN؟

If you don't have a Social Security Number (SSN) and match any of the following situations, you likely need an ITIN to file your U.S. taxes:

👤 Non-Resident Aliens with U.S. Income

If you're not a U.S. citizen, not a permanent resident (no green card), but earned income from a U.S. source (wages, self-employment, investments, rental property), you need an ITIN to report and pay taxes on that income.

لو ما عندك citizenship ولا green card بس دخلك من بزنس أو فايدة أمريكية، بتحتاج ITIN.

💑 Spouse of a U.S. Citizen (Foreign Spouse)

A foreign national married to a U.S. citizen can elect to file jointly using an ITIN. This allows the couple to benefit from better tax brackets and credits.

الزوج/الزوجة الأجنبي/ة لمواطن أمريكي يقدر يقدم إقرار مشترك باستخدام ITIN ويستفيد من خصومات أفضل.

👶 Dependent with No SSN

A child or dependent of a U.S. citizen or permanent resident who has no Social Security Number (often newly arrived or adopted from abroad) must use an ITIN for the parent to claim them as a dependent.

الطفل أو المعيل اللي ما عنده SSN (وصل حديث أو تم تبنيه) بيحتاج ITIN عشان الوالد يدعيه كمعيل.

📄 Student with Work-Study or On-Campus Job

An international student working on-campus (allowed under F-1 visa) or in authorized work-study must file taxes and needs an ITIN if they don't have an SSN.

الطالب الدولي اللي يشتغل في الجامعة بتصريح بيحتاج ITIN لتقديم إقراره الضريبي.

Who Does NOT Need an ITIN

U.S. Citizens: If you're a U.S. citizen, you have an SSN and don't need an ITIN. Permanent Residents (Green Card Holders): Green card holders have an SSN or can get one. People with No U.S. Income: If you earned no U.S. income and have no U.S. tax filing requirement, you don't need an ITIN.

المواطنين الأمريكيين عندهم SSN. الـ green card holders عندهم أو يقدرو يحصلو على SSN. اللي ما عندهم دخل أمريكي ما يحتاجون ITIN.

Chapter 3

ITIN vs. Social Security Number (SSN)

الفرق بين ITIN و SSN

While both ITIN and SSN are nine-digit numbers used for tax identification, they serve different purposes and have significant legal differences.

Feature ITIN (9XX-XX-XXXX) SSN (Not 9)
Issued By Internal Revenue Service (IRS) Social Security Administration (SSA)
For Tax Filing Only ✓ Yes, tax purposes only ✗ No, multi-purpose
Can Work in U.S. ✗ No, not without authorization ✓ Yes (with work permit)
Social Security Benefits ✗ No eligibility ✓ Yes, after qualifying
Medicare / Medicaid ✗ Limited eligibility ✓ Full eligibility
File Joint Return ✓ Yes, with citizen spouse ✓ Yes
Claim Dependents ✓ Yes, limited ✓ Yes
Bank Account ✗ Not typically accepted ✓ Yes, required
⚠️
Critical Difference

Do NOT use an ITIN as an SSN. If you obtain a valid SSN later (through a green card, citizenship, or work visa), you must switch to your SSN immediately. Using both numbers on your taxes can trigger IRS audits.

لا تخلط بين ITIN و SSN. لو حصلت على SSN لاحقاً، بدّل إلى SSN فوراً. استخدام الاثنين معاً على الضرائب بيسبب تحقيق من الـ IRS.

Can You Get Both ITIN and SSN?

Yes, but you should not use both simultaneously for taxes. If you apply for an SSN (through employment or permanent residency) after already having an ITIN, you must report the new SSN to the IRS and use it going forward. Having both numbers on file is fine; using both on tax returns is problematic.

تقدر تحصل عليهما لكن ما تستخدم الاثنين معاً للضرائب. لو حصلت على SSN بعدما كان عندك ITIN، أخبر الـ IRS باستخدم SSN بعدها.

Chapter 4

How to Apply for ITIN (Form W-7)

كيفية التقديم للحصول على ITIN (فورم W-7)

To apply for an ITIN, you must complete Form W-7 (Application for IRS Individual Taxpayer Identification Number) and submit it to the IRS with your supporting documents. The process takes 4–6 weeks.

1

Gather Your Documents

Collect your passport, foreign ID, visa, or travel documents that prove your identity and foreign status. You'll need original documents or certified copies.

اجمع جوازك، هويتك الأجنبية، أو تأشيرتك. بتحتاج أصلية أو صورة معتمدة.

2

Complete Form W-7

Fill out Form W-7 completely. Include your name (as shown on your passport), date of birth, current address, and date of arrival in the U.S. (if applicable).

عبّي فورم W-7 كاملاً. ضع اسمك كما يظهر في جوازك، تاريخ ميلادك، عنوانك الحالي.

3

Choose Submission Method

Option A: Mail Form W-7 with documents and tax return to the IRS. Option B: Use an IRS Acceptance Agent (bank, credit union, tax professional) who can verify your documents in person. Option C: ITIN applicant whose principal reason is to file a U.S. tax return can attach Form W-7 to their tax return.

بتقدر تبعت بالبريد، تروح لوكيل معتمد (بنك أو محاسب)، أو ترفقها بإقرارك الضريبي.

4

Mail or Submit In Person

Send your application to your local IRS office or the IRS ITIN processing center. Processing typically takes 4–6 weeks. You can check the status online using Form W-7 tracking.

أرسل الفورم للـ IRS. المعالجة تأخذ ٤-٦ أسابيع. تقدر تتابع الحالة أونلاين.

5

Receive Your ITIN

The IRS will mail you a letter with your new ITIN (usually within 4–6 weeks). Your ITIN is valid for federal tax purposes immediately.

الـ IRS بترسل لك خطاب فيه ITIN جديد. ITIN فوري الاستخدام للضرائب.

Processing Times & Status

ITIN application processing typically takes 4–6 weeks. During peak tax season (January–April), processing can take longer. You can track your application status using the IRS ITIN tracker on IRS.gov. You'll need your Social Security Number (or ITIN if already assigned), filing status, and expected refund amount.

المعالجة تأخذ ٤-٦ أسابيع عادة. في موسم الضرائب (يناير-أبريل) قد تأخذ أطول. تقدر تتابع أونلاين على IRS.gov.

💡 Pro Tip: Acceptance Agents

Using an IRS-authorized Acceptance Agent (bank, credit union, or tax firm) is often the fastest method. They verify your documents in person and submit immediately, reducing processing time.

استخدام وكيل معتمد (بنك أو محاسب) أسرع. هم يتحققون من وثائقك مباشرة ويرسلونها فوراً.

Chapter 5

Required Documents for ITIN Application

المستندات المطلوبة لطلب ITIN

When submitting Form W-7, the IRS requires you to provide proof of your identity and foreign status. Here are all acceptable documents:

Proof of Identity (Choose 1)

🆔 Acceptable ID Documents
  • Valid passport (most common and preferred)
  • Foreign national ID card (government-issued)
  • Driver's license (from your home country)
  • Travel visa (showing your name and photo)
  • Military ID (from your home country)
  • National civil registration ID

جواز السفر (الأفضل)، الهوية الحكومية، رخصة السيارة، التأشيرة، هوية عسكرية، أو بطاقة تسجيل مدني.

Proof of Foreign Status (Choose 1)

📋 Foreign Status Documents
  • Valid passport (proves you're a foreign national)
  • Foreign birth certificate (with certified English translation if not in English)
  • Travel visa (U.S. visa or visa from another country)
  • Immigration documents (arrival/departure records, I-94)
  • Foreign government ID (civil ID, national ID card)
  • Divorce decree or marriage certificate (from foreign country, with translation)

جواز السفر، شهادة الميلاد الأجنبية، تأشيرة، وثائق الهجرة، هوية حكومية أجنبية.

For Self-Employment Income (Schedule C Filers)

If you're applying for an ITIN because you have self-employment income, you must also include:

  • EIN Letter (Employer Identification Number) — if you have a business
  • Business license or registration documents
  • Partnership agreement (if applicable)
  • Evidence of business activity (invoices, contracts, bank statements)
⚠️
Important Requirements

Original or Certified Copies Only: The IRS requires original documents or copies certified by the issuing government agency. Photocopies alone are not acceptable. English Translation Required: Any foreign documents must include a certified English translation (prepared by a professional translator).

الـ IRS تطلب أصلية أو صورة معتمدة من الجهة الحكومية. الفوتو كوبي ما يكفي. أي مستند أجنبي بيحتاج ترجمة معتمدة.

How to Submit Documents

By Mail: Send original documents or certified copies via registered mail to your local IRS office. Keep a copy for your records. In Person: Visit an IRS office or an IRS-authorized Acceptance Agent (bank, credit union, tax firm) to have your documents verified and scanned. With Your Tax Return: Attach copies to your Form 1040 when filing.

بتقدر تبعت بالبريد المسجل، تروح شخصياً للـ IRS أو وكيل معتمد، أو ترفقها بإقرارك.

Chapter 6

ITIN Renewal Process

تجديد رقم ITIN

ITINs don't last forever. The IRS automatically suspends ITINs if there's been no tax return filed using that ITIN for three consecutive years. If your ITIN is about to expire or has expired, you must renew it to continue filing taxes.

When Does Your ITIN Expire?

Your ITIN is valid indefinitely as long as you file a U.S. tax return every year using that ITIN. However, the IRS suspends ITINs that haven't been used on a tax return for three consecutive years. If you haven't filed in that time, your ITIN may be deactivated.

ITIN صالح للأبد طالما تقدم إقرار ضريبي سنوي. لكن الـ IRS توقف ITIN لو ما قدمت إقرار لثلاث سنين متتالية.

📅
Suspension Rules

No filing for 3 years? Your ITIN is suspended. Already suspended? You can renew it using Form W-7. Renewal is similar to initial application. After suspension: Once renewed, file your tax return within 6 months to keep it active.

ثلاث سنين بلا تقديم = توقف ITIN. التجديد مثل الطلب الأول. بعد التجديد، قدم إقرار خلال ٦ أشهر.

How to Renew Your ITIN

1

Check Your ITIN Status

Visit IRS.gov or call 1-800-829-1040 to verify if your ITIN is active, suspended, or expired.

2

Complete Form W-7 (Renewal)

Fill out Form W-7 again with your current information. Check the box for "Renewal" instead of "Initial Request." Include your existing ITIN on the form.

عبّي فورم W-7 مرة ثانية وضع علامة بـ "Renewal" بدل "Initial Request".

3

Provide Updated Documents

You may need to provide renewed documentation of identity and foreign status. If your passport is about to expire, renew it first. Use current, valid documents.

4

Submit Renewal with Tax Return

The easiest way is to file your tax return and attach Form W-7 (renewal) with your supporting documents. This counts as your renewed ITIN and your tax filing in one submission.

أسهل طريقة: قدم إقرار ضريبي وارفق فورم W-7 معاه وثائقك.

✓
Best Practice

File your tax return every year, even if you don't owe taxes or don't have much income. Filing annually keeps your ITIN active and prevents suspension. It also ensures you get any refundable credits you're entitled to.

قدم إقرار كل سنة حتى لو ما عليك ضرائب. هذا بيحافظ على ITIN نشط ويساعدك تحصل على أي ائتمانات ترجع كاش.

Chapter 7

Resident vs. Non-Resident Alien Filing Status

حالة المقيم مقابل غير المقيم الأجنبي

The most critical decision for ITIN filers is determining whether you're a Resident Alien or a Non-Resident Alien for tax purposes. This status determines which tax form you file, what income is taxable, and which deductions and credits you can claim.

🏠 Resident Alien (RA)

A resident alien is a foreign national who meets the Green Card Test or the Substantial Presence Test.

Green Card Test: You have a valid green card (permanent resident status). Substantial Presence Test: You've been in the U.S. for 183 days or more during a 3-year period (current year + 2 prior years), with a weighted calculation. Resident aliens are taxed like U.S. citizens on worldwide income.

المقيم الأجنبي عنده green card أو قضى ١٨٣ يوم أو أكثر بأمريكا. يتحسب دخله العالمي مثل المواطن الأمريكي.

🌍 Non-Resident Alien (NRA)

A non-resident alien is a foreign national who fails both the Green Card Test and the Substantial Presence Test. Non-residents are only taxed on U.S.-source income (wages earned in the U.S., business income from a U.S. business, rental income from U.S. property, certain investment income).

غير المقيم الأجنبي فقط يدفع ضرائب على الدخل من مصادر أمريكية. الدخل من خارج أمريكا ما بيتحسب.

Tax Forms: Which One Do I File?

StatusTax FormWorldwide Income Taxed?
Resident AlienForm 1040Yes, all worldwide income
Non-Resident AlienForm 1040-NRNo, U.S.-source only

Electing Resident Alien Status

Some non-residents with a foreign spouse can make an election to be treated as a resident alien. This allows them to file a joint return with their U.S. spouse (or citizen spouse) and access better tax rates and credits. This election is made on Form 8288-B and applies for the entire tax year.

بعض غير المقيمين مع زوج/زوجة أجنبي يقدرو يختارو معاملتهم كمقيم ويقدمو إقرار مشترك بتصريح أفضل.

⚠️
Critical: Determine Your Status Correctly

Filing under the wrong status can trigger IRS audits and penalties. Use the IRS Publication 519 (Tax Guide for Aliens) to calculate your residency status. When in doubt, consult a tax professional familiar with ITIN filers.

اختيار الحالة الخاطئة بيسبب مشاكل. استخدم IRS Publication 519 أو استشر محاسب مختص بـ ITIN.

Chapter 8

Tax Credits & Limitations for ITIN Holders

الائتمانات الضريبية والقيود على حاملي ITIN

ITIN holders have different eligibility for tax credits compared to SSN holders. Some credits are restricted, while others are fully available. Understanding these rules is crucial for maximizing your tax benefits.

Fully Available Credits (No ITIN Restrictions)

✓ Education Credits

American Opportunity Credit: Up to $2,500/year (first 4 years of post-secondary education). Lifetime Learning Credit: Up to $2,000/year (any post-secondary education). ITIN holders can claim both if they have qualified education expenses.

ائتمان الفرصة الأمريكية لحد ٢,٥٠٠$ سنويا. ائتمان التعلم المستمر لحد ٢,٠٠٠$. حاملو ITIN يقدرو يدعوهم.

✓ Mortgage Interest Deduction

If you own a home and have a mortgage, you can deduct your mortgage interest (up to $750,000 of mortgage debt). ITIN holders can claim this deduction if they meet residency requirements.

خصم فوائد الرهن العقاري متاح للجميع إذا كنت مالك منزل.

RESTRICTED: Child Tax Credit (CTC)

❌
Major Restriction

ITIN holders cannot claim the regular Child Tax Credit. Instead, only dependents with SSN or ITIN can be included on your return. If your child has an ITIN, you cannot claim the $2,000 per child tax credit. However, you can claim the dependent as an exemption (no longer reduces taxable income directly, but may qualify for other benefits).

حاملو ITIN لا يقدرو يدعو CTC للأطفال. لكن يقدرو يدعوهم كمعيلين إذا كان عندهم SSN أو ITIN خاص.

RESTRICTED: Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC)

The EITC is one of the largest refundable credits available to low-income workers. However, ITIN holders are generally NOT eligible for EITC unless they are married filing jointly with a U.S. citizen or permanent resident spouse who has an SSN.

حاملو ITIN عادة ما يقدرو يدعو EITC إلا إذا كانوا متزوجين ويقدمون إقرار مشترك مع زوج مواطن أو green card holder.

💑 Exception: Married ITIN Holders

If you're married to a U.S. citizen or green card holder and both of you have Social Security Numbers (or one has SSN and the other has ITIN), you can file a joint return and claim EITC. However, both spouses must have valid ITINs or SSNs on the return.

إذا كنت متزوج من مواطن أمريكي أو green card holder، تقدرو تقدموا إقرار مشترك والدعي EITC.

Available: Certain Tax Deductions

Standard Deduction: ITIN holders can claim the standard deduction ($15,000 single, $30,000 married filing jointly in 2025). Itemized Deductions: Mortgage interest, state and local taxes (up to $10,000), charitable donations, medical expenses (over 7.5% of AGI). Business Deductions: If you have self-employment income, you can deduct business expenses (home office, vehicle mileage, supplies, professional fees).

الخصم المعياري متاح، الخصومات المفصلة (فائدة الرهن، الضرائب المحلية، التبرعات)، خصومات البزنس — كلها متاحة لحاملي ITIN.

📋
Record Deductions Carefully

Keep detailed records of all deductible expenses: receipts for charity, mortgage statements, property tax bills, and business expense logs. The IRS audits ITIN filers at higher rates, so documentation is essential.

احفظ إيصالات التبرعات، فاتورات الرهن، ضرائب الممتلكات، سجلات مصاريف البزنس. الـ IRS تفتش حاملي ITIN أكثر.

Chapter 9

Tax Treaty Benefits

فوائد معاهدات الضرائب

If your country has a tax treaty with the United States, you may qualify for reduced tax rates on certain types of income (interest, dividends, royalties, etc.). Treaty benefits can significantly reduce your U.S. tax liability.

What Are Tax Treaties?

Tax treaties are agreements between two countries to avoid double taxation. They allow residents of one country to pay tax only in their home country on certain types of income, rather than paying tax in both countries. The U.S. has treaties with over 60 countries.

معاهدات الضرائب اتفاقيات بين بلدين لتجنب الضريبة المزدوجة. تسمح لك تدفع ضريبة بدولة واحدة بدل اثنين.

Common Treaty Benefits

📊 Reduced Withholding Rates

Interest Income (1099-INT): Usually reduced from 30% to 0-15% withholding. Dividend Income (1099-DIV): Usually reduced from 30% to 5-15% withholding. Royalty Income: Usually reduced from 30% to 0-10% withholding. Student Loan Interest: Often 0% withholding for students of certain countries.

الضريبة على الفائدة قد تنخفض من ٣٠٪ إلى ٠-١٥٪. الأرباح من ٣٠٪ إلى ٥-١٥٪. الرسوم من ٣٠٪ إلى ٠-١٠٪.

How to Claim Treaty Benefits

1

Verify Your Country's Treaty

Check the IRS website (IRS.gov/treaties) to see if your country has a tax treaty with the U.S. and which income types are covered.

2

File Form W-8BEN (for non-residents)

If you're a non-resident alien claiming treaty benefits, provide your employer or bank with Form W-8BEN (Certificate of Eligibility). This tells them you're a treaty resident and entitled to reduced withholding.

أعطِ صاحب العمل أو البنك فورم W-8BEN عشان يقللوا الضريبة المخصومة.

3

Claim Benefits on Your Tax Return

When filing your Form 1040 or 1040-NR, you can claim treaty benefits on Schedule 3. Attach a copy of your treaty eligibility certificate (country of origin letter or passport).

💰
Potential Refund

If excessive taxes were withheld and you claim treaty benefits, you can file an amended return (Form 1040-X) to reclaim the overpaid taxes. Many ITIN holders recover hundreds or thousands in overpaid taxes through treaty claims.

لو انخصمت ضرائب زيادة وأنت مستحق فوائد معاهدة، تقدر تقدم إقرار معدل وترجع الضرائب الزائدة.

List of U.S. Tax Treaties (Sample)

The U.S. has comprehensive tax treaties with countries including: Canada, Mexico, United Kingdom, Germany, France, Australia, Japan, India, China, Brazil, Egypt, United Arab Emirates, Saudi Arabia, Israel, South Korea, and many others. Check IRS.gov for a complete, current list of all 60+ treaty countries.

الـ US معاها معاهدات ضرائب مع أكثر من ٦٠ دولة. شوف IRS.gov للقائمة الكاملة والحالية.

Chapter 10

Common Mistakes ITIN Filers Make

الأخطاء الشائعة التي يرتكبها حاملو ITIN

ITIN filers often make preventable mistakes that trigger audits, rejections, or missed refunds. Here are the most common errors and how to avoid them:

Mistake #1: Using Wrong Tax Form

❌ Filing Form 1040 as a Non-Resident Alien

Non-resident aliens must file Form 1040-NR, not Form 1040. Filing the wrong form can cause the IRS to reject your return, delay your refund, or trigger an audit. Verify your residency status before choosing a form.

غير المقيمين لازم يقدموا 1040-NR وليس 1040. الفورم الخاطئ بيسبب رفض أو تحقيق.

Mistake #2: Claiming Credits You're Not Eligible For

❌ Claiming Child Tax Credit (CTC)

ITIN holders cannot claim the CTC. Many filers try anyway, hoping the IRS won't notice. The IRS always catches this error, and you'll receive a notice requesting the credit amount back. Don't claim CTC if you have an ITIN.

لا تحاول تدعي CTC لو كان عندك ITIN. الـ IRS بتكتشفها دايماً وبترد الفلوس.

Mistake #3: Not Renewing an Expired ITIN

If your ITIN has been suspended due to inactivity (3+ years without filing), you cannot file a return until you renew it. Many people discover their ITIN is expired only when they try to file. Renew proactively if you haven't filed in 2+ years.

لا تنسى تجدد ITIN إذا ما قدمت إقرار لسنتين أو أكثر. الـ IRS بتوقف الرقم تلقائياً.

Mistake #4: Switching Between ITIN and SSN Without Notifying IRS

If you obtain an SSN after having an ITIN (through a green card, work visa, or citizenship), you must stop using the ITIN and file with the SSN. Continuing to use both numbers creates a discrepancy that triggers IRS audits and penalties.

لو حصلت على SSN بعدما كان عندك ITIN، بدّل إلى SSN فوراً. استخدام الرقمين معاً يسبب مشاكل.

Mistake #5: Incorrect ITIN on Joint Return

If you're filing a joint return with your spouse, make sure both ITINs (or SSN/ITIN combination) are entered correctly. A single digit error causes the return to be rejected. Double-check the ITIN letter you received from the IRS.

في الإقرار المشترك، تأكد الأرقام صحيحة. حتى خطأ برقم واحد بيرفع الإقرار.

Chapter 11

State Income Taxes for ITIN Holders

ضرائب الولاية لحاملي ITIN

In addition to federal taxes, some states require you to file a state income tax return if you earn income within their borders or live in a state with income tax. ITIN requirements vary by state.

States That Require ITIN for State Taxes

Most states that have an income tax require a valid ITIN, SSN, or ATIN (Adoption Taxpayer Identification Number) to file. A few states are flexible:

✓ States Accepting ITIN

California, New York, Illinois, Massachusetts, Maryland, New Jersey, Connecticut, Rhode Island — all accept ITIN for state income tax filing. New Mexico, Colorado — accept ITIN with some restrictions. Check your state's tax authority website for current rules.

معظم الولايات التي فيها ضريبة دخل تقبل ITIN. كاليفورنيا، نيويورك، إلينوي، وغيرها تقبل ITIN بدون مشاكل.

States With NO Income Tax

If you live in a state with no income tax, you don't need to file a state return. These states include: Alaska, Florida, Nevada, South Dakota, Tennessee, Texas, Washington, Wyoming. (Note: Some of these have other taxes like capital gains or sales tax, but no income tax.)

الولايات بدون ضريبة دخل: ألاسكا، فلوريدا، نيفادا، تكساس، وايومينغ وغيرها. ما بتحتاج تقدم state return.

How to File State Taxes with ITIN

Step 1: Determine if your state has income tax and if it applies to you. Step 2: Obtain a state ITIN if required (some states issue their own ITINs; others use federal ITIN). Step 3: File your state return using the same information as your federal return. Step 4: Pay any state tax owed or claim a refund.

تأكد إذا كانت ولايتك فيها ضريبة دخل وإذا بتطبق عليك. احصل على ITIN الولاية (لو لازم). قدم إقرار الولاية. ادفع أو احصل على مرتجع.

State-Specific Guidance

State tax rules for ITIN holders are complex and vary. We recommend consulting your state's department of revenue or a local tax professional to confirm your filing obligations. Many states have special rules for non-residents and immigrants.

القوانين تختلف من ولاية لأخرى. استشر قسم الضرائب بالولاية أو محاسب محلي للتأكد من التزاماتك.
