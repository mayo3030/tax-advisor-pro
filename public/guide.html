Chapter 1

How U.S. Taxes Work

كيف تشتغل الضرائب الأمريكية

The United States has a progressive income tax system. This means you don't pay the same tax rate on all your income. Instead, different portions of your income are taxed at different rates, from 10% up to 37%. النظام الضريبي الأمريكي تصاعدي — يعني مش كل دخلك يتحسب بنفس النسبة. كل شريحة من دخلك لها نسبة مختلفة.

🏛️ The Big Picture

Every year, you file a tax return (Form 1040) to tell the IRS how much you earned, how much you already paid in taxes, and whether you owe more or get a refund.

كل سنة بتعبي فورم 1040 عشان تقول للـ IRS كم كسبت، كم دفعت ضرائب، واذا لازم تدفع أكثر أو بترجعلك فلوس.

Example

If you earn $60,000, you don't pay 22% on all of it. You pay 10% on the first $11,925, then 12% on the next chunk, then 22% on the rest. Your effective rate ends up around 14%.

لو دخلك ٦٠ ألف، مش كله بيتحسب ٢٢٪. كل شريحة بتتحسب لحالها. النسبة الفعلية بتطلع تقريبا ١٤٪.

Chapter 2

Income Types

أنواع الدخل

The IRS cares about all your income. Here are the two most common types for most people:

📄 W-2 Income (Employee)

If you work for a company, you get a W-2 form every January. It shows your total wages, federal taxes withheld, Social Security, and Medicare taxes. Your employer already deducted taxes from each paycheck.

لو تشتغل عند شركة، بتوصلك ورقة W-2 بشهر يناير. فيها كل دخلك، والضرائب اللي انخصمت من راتبك.

📋 1099-NEC Income (Self-Employed / Freelance)

If you do freelance work, drive for Uber, or have your own business, clients send you a 1099-NEC. No taxes are deducted, so you owe both income tax AND self-employment tax (15.3% for Social Security + Medicare).

لو تشتغل حر (فريلانس) أو عندك بزنس، بتوصلك ورقة 1099. ما في ضرائب مخصومة — لازم تدفع ضريبة الدخل + ضريبة العمل الحر (١٥.٣٪).

⚠️
Important — مهم

If you have 1099 income, you may need to pay quarterly estimated taxes (every 3 months) to avoid penalties. The dates are: April 15, June 15, September 15, and January 15.

لو عندك دخل من 1099، لازم تدفع ضرائب تقديرية كل ٣ شهور عشان ما تتفاجأ بغرامات.

Other Income Types

Interest income (1099-INT) from bank accounts, investment income (1099-B/1099-DIV) from stocks and dividends, rental income from property, and retirement distributions (1099-R) are all taxable and must be reported.

دخل الفوائد من البنك، دخل الأسهم، دخل الإيجار، وسحب التقاعد — كلهم لازم تبلّغ عنهم.

Chapter 3

Filing Status

حالة التقديم

Your filing status affects your tax brackets, standard deduction, and which credits you qualify for. Choose the one that fits your situation on December 31 of the tax year.

Chapter 4

Deductions

الخصومات — تقلل دخلك الخاضع للضريبة

Deductions reduce your taxable income. Less taxable income means less tax. You choose between the standard deduction (a fixed amount) or itemized deductions (listing individual expenses).

📊 Standard vs. Itemized

Standard Deduction: A fixed dollar amount ($15,000 for single, $30,000 for married filing jointly in 2025). No receipts needed. Most people use this because it's higher than their itemized total.

Itemized Deductions: You list specific expenses: mortgage interest, medical bills over 7.5% of income, state/local taxes (max $10,000), and charitable donations. Only choose this if your total exceeds the standard deduction.

الخصم المعياري: مبلغ ثابت بدون ما تحتاج إيصالات. الخصم المفصّل: تعدّ كل مصاريفك. اختار اللي يعطيك خصم أكبر.

Business Expenses (Schedule C)

If you have 1099 self-employment income, you can deduct business expenses before calculating your tax. Common deductions include:

💼
Common Business Deductions

Home Office: $5 per square foot (up to 300 sq ft) = max $1,500. Or calculate actual expenses.

Vehicle: 70 cents per business mile in 2025. Keep a mileage log!

Supplies: Computer, software, tools, printer — anything used for your business.

Phone/Internet: The business percentage of your monthly bill.

Professional Services: Accountant fees, legal fees, subscriptions.

مكتب بالبيت، سيارة، أدوات، تلفون وإنترنت، خدمات محاسبية — كلها تنخصم من دخل الفريلانس.

Above-the-Line Deductions

These special deductions reduce your income even if you take the standard deduction: IRA contributions (up to $7,000), Student loan interest (up to $2,500), HSA contributions ($4,300 individual / $8,550 family), and Self-employed health insurance premiums.

هاي الخصومات تنخصم حتى لو اخترت الخصم المعياري — تقاعد IRA، فوائد قرض الطالب، حساب صحي HSA، وتأمين صحي للعاملين لحالهم.

Chapter 5

Tax Credits

الائتمانات الضريبية — تقلل الضريبة مباشرة

Credits are even better than deductions. A deduction reduces your income, but a credit reduces your actual tax bill dollar-for-dollar. A $2,000 credit saves you $2,000.

الائتمانات أحسن من الخصومات! الائتمان ينقص من الضريبة مباشرة — يعني ائتمان ٢٠٠٠$ = ٢٠٠٠$ أقل ضرائب.

👶 Child Tax Credit

Up to $2,000 per child under 17. If your income is under $200,000 (single) or $400,000 (married), you likely qualify. Up to $1,700 is refundable, meaning you can get it even if you owe $0 in taxes.

لحد ٢٠٠٠$ لكل طفل تحت ١٧ سنة. لحد ١٧٠٠$ ممكن ترجعلك حتى لو ما عليك ضرائب.

💰 Earned Income Tax Credit (EITC)

For low-to-moderate income workers. Worth up to $7,830 with 3+ children. Fully refundable. Income limits depend on filing status and number of children.

للعمال بدخل منخفض أو متوسط. ممكن توصل لـ ٧,٨٣٠$ — كلها ترجعلك كاش!

🎓 Education Credits

American Opportunity Credit: Up to $2,500/year for the first 4 years of college. Lifetime Learning Credit: Up to $2,000/year for any post-secondary education.

ائتمان الجامعة لحد ٢,٥٠٠$ لأول ٤ سنين. ائتمان التعلم المستمر لحد ٢,٠٠٠$ لأي تعليم بعد الثانوية.

🔋 Energy Credits

Bought a new electric vehicle? You may get up to $7,500. Installed solar panels? Up to 30% of the cost. Made energy-efficient home improvements? Up to $3,200.

سيارة كهربائية لحد ٧,٥٠٠$. ألواح شمسية ٣٠٪ من التكلفة. تحسينات طاقة بالبيت لحد ٣,٢٠٠$.

Chapter 6

Filing & Deadlines

مواعيد التقديم والدفع
📅
Key Date

The deadline to file your 2025 tax return is April 15, 2026. If you can't make it, file for an automatic 6-month extension (Form 4868) — but you must still pay what you owe by April 15.

آخر موعد لتقديم إقرار ٢٠٢٥ هو ١٥ أبريل ٢٠٢٦. ممكن تمدد ٦ شهور بس لازم تدفع بالموعد.

What Happens if You Owe?

If your tax return shows you owe money, you can pay online at IRS.gov/payments. The IRS also offers installment plans if you can't pay all at once. Penalties for late filing (5% per month) are steeper than late payment (0.5% per month), so always file on time even if you can't pay.

لو طلع عليك مبلغ، ادفع أونلاين. الـ IRS يقبل أقساط. غرامة التأخر بالتقديم أكبر من التأخر بالدفع — قدّم بالوقت دايماً!

What Happens if You Get a Refund?

If you overpaid taxes during the year, the IRS sends you a refund. Most refunds arrive within 21 days if you file electronically and choose direct deposit. You can check your status at IRS.gov/refunds.

لو دفعت ضرائب زيادة، الـ IRS يرجعلك الفرق. أغلب المرتجعات توصل خلال ٢١ يوم لو قدمت إلكتروني.

Chapter 7

Getting Started

كيف تبدأ مع التطبيق

Tax Advisor Pro runs on your own computer. No data goes to any third party except the AI provider (Anthropic/Claude) for answering your questions. Your tax data stays in a local database on your machine.

التطبيق يشتغل على جهازك. ما في بيانات تروح لأي جهة غير الـ AI. بياناتك الضريبية تنحفظ على جهازك بس.

1

Double-click start.bat

Open the tax-advisor-pro folder and double-click start.bat. A black command window opens. Wait for the message: "Ready! Open http://localhost:3000 in your browser."

افتح مجلد tax-advisor-pro ودبل كلك على start.bat. استنى لما يقولك "Ready".

2

Open Chrome

Go to http://localhost:3000 in Google Chrome. The app loads with all 9 steps visible in the top progress bar.

افتح كروم وروح على localhost:3000. التطبيق يفتح ومعاه ٩ خطوات بشريط التقدم.

3

Keep the black window open

The command window is your server. Closing it stops the app. Minimize it if you want, but don't close it while working.

النافذة السوداء هي السيرفر. لا تسكرها! صغّرها بس خليها مفتوحة.

🔑
API Key

The AI chatbot needs a Claude API key. It's stored in the .env file inside the tax-advisor-pro folder. If the chatbot says "API key invalid", open .env with Notepad and update the key.

الشات بوت يحتاج مفتاح API. المفتاح محفوظ بملف .env. لو الشات قال "مفتاح غلط"، عدّل الملف بالنوتباد.

The 9 Steps

الخطوات التسعة لتعبئة إقرارك

The app guides you through 9 steps, from uploading documents to reviewing your final return. You can go back to any completed step to make changes.

0

Welcome أهلاً

Introduction and overview. Just click "Get Started" to begin.

1

Upload Documents رفع الأوراق

Drag and drop your W-2s, 1099s, or any tax documents. The AI can scan them and auto-fill your forms. Supports PDF and images (photos of paper documents).

ارفع أوراقك — W-2، 1099، أي مستند ضريبي. الذكاء الاصطناعي يقرأهم ويعبي الفورمات تلقائي!

2

Personal Information معلومات شخصية

Enter your full name, Social Security Number, date of birth, and address. This information goes on the top of your Form 1040.

3

Filing Status حالة التقديم

Choose: Single, Married Filing Jointly, Married Filing Separately, or Head of Household. If you have dependents (children), add them here too.

4

W-2 Income دخل الوظيفة

Enter each W-2 form: employer name, total wages (Box 1), federal tax withheld (Box 2), Social Security wages (Box 3), Medicare wages (Box 5), and state tax (Box 17). Click "Add W-2" for multiple employers.

5

1099 Income دخل الفريلانس

Enter 1099-NEC forms: payer/client name, amount received, and EIN. Below that, enter your business expenses (home office, vehicle, supplies, etc.).

Choose standard or itemized deduction. If itemized, enter medical expenses, state/local taxes (SALT), mortgage interest, and charitable donations. Also enter above-the-line deductions (IRA, student loans, HSA).

Select any credits that apply to you: Child Tax Credit, EITC, Education, Child Care, Saver's Credit, or Energy credits. The app checks eligibility based on your data.

8

See your complete tax calculation: total income, deductions, taxable income, tax owed, credits applied, and whether you get a refund or owe. Download your return as a PDF.

شوف النتيجة النهائية — كم دخلك، كم الخصومات، كم الضريبة، واذا بترجعلك فلوس أو عليك مبلغ. حمّل كـ PDF.

Chapter 9

The AI Chatbot

المستشار الذكي — شات بوت الضرائب

The chatbot (💬 button at the bottom-right) is your personal tax advisor. It speaks English and Arabic, knows U.S. tax law, and — most importantly — it sees everything on your screen.

الشات بوت (زر 💬 تحت يمين) هو مستشارك الضريبي. يحكي إنجليزي وعربي، ويعرف قانون الضرائب — والأهم: يشوف كل بياناتك على الموقع!

🧠 Context-Aware

The AI has live access to all your form data: personal info, income, deductions, credits, uploaded documents, and even extracted text from scanned files. Ask it anything about YOUR specific situation.

الذكاء الاصطناعي يشوف كل بياناتك لحظة بلحظة — معلوماتك الشخصية، دخلك، خصوماتك، المستندات المرفوعة. اسأله أي شي عن وضعك!

Two Modes

🎙️ Tax Interview Mode

Click "Start Tax Interview" and the AI conducts a face-to-face meeting — asking about your income, deductions, dependents, and credits one question at a time. At the end, click "Fill All My Forms" and it auto-fills everything.

اضغط "ابدأ المقابلة" والـ AI يسألك سؤال سؤال زي المحاسب الحقيقي. بالنهاية يعبي كل الفورمات تلقائي!

💬 Free Chat Mode

Just type any question: "What deductions can I take?", "Am I eligible for EITC?", "How much is my refund?", "ممكن تشرحلي W-2?" — the AI gives personalized answers based on your actual data.

Uploading Files in Chat

Click the 📎 button next to the text input to attach images or documents directly in the chat. You can send photos of W-2s, 1099s, receipts, or any tax document. The AI can read and discuss them with you.

اضغط 📎 عشان ترفق صور أو مستندات بالشات مباشرة. ارسل صور W-2 أو إيصالات والـ AI يقرأهم ويناقشهم معك.

Chat History

All messages are automatically saved. When you reopen the chat, your previous conversation loads back. Use the 🗑 Clear button to start fresh if needed.

كل الرسائل تنحفظ تلقائي. لما تفتح الشات مرة ثانية، المحادثة السابقة تطلع. اضغط "مسح" لو تبي تبدأ من جديد.

Chapter 10

Upload & Auto-Scan

رفع المستندات والمسح التلقائي

The upload feature (Step 1) is one of the most powerful parts of the app. Here's how to use it:

1

Upload your documents

Drag files into the upload zone or click to browse. Supports: PDF files, photos (JPG/PNG), and images of paper documents.

2

Click "Scan & Auto-Fill"

The AI reads each document: extracts text from PDFs, reads numbers from photos using vision AI. It identifies W-2 fields, 1099 amounts, and other data.

3

Review the auto-filled forms

Go through each step and verify the numbers the AI entered. Always double-check — AI is accurate but not perfect.

راجع الأرقام اللي الـ AI عبّاها. دايماً تأكد — الذكاء الاصطناعي دقيق بس مش كامل.

📸
Photo Tips

For best results: use good lighting, take the photo straight-on (not angled), and make sure all numbers are legible. The AI handles both printed and some handwritten documents.

للنتائج الأفضل: إضاءة جيدة، صوّر مباشرة (مش من زاوية)، تأكد الأرقام واضحة.

Chapter 11

Tips & FAQ

نصائح وأسئلة شائعة
❓ The chatbot says "Connection error"

Make sure the black command window (start.bat) is still open and running. If it closed, double-click start.bat again. Then refresh Chrome (F5).

تأكد النافذة السوداء لسا مفتوحة. لو انسكرت، شغّل start.bat مرة ثانية وحدّث الصفحة.

❓ Can I save my work and come back later?

Yes! Your data is saved automatically to a local database. When you reopen localhost:3000, your session and chat history will be there.

أيوا! بياناتك تنحفظ تلقائي. لما ترجع وتفتح localhost:3000 كل شي بيكون موجود.

❓ Is my data secure?

Your tax data stays on your computer in a local database file. The only external connection is to Claude AI (Anthropic) for chatbot answers. No data is stored on any cloud server.

بياناتك على جهازك بس. الاتصال الوحيد الخارجي هو مع Claude AI للإجابات. ما في بيانات تنحفظ على أي سيرفر.

❓ Can I use this to actually file my taxes?

This app helps you prepare your return and understand your tax situation. To officially file with the IRS, you'll need to submit through an IRS-approved e-file provider or print and mail your forms.

التطبيق يساعدك تجهّز إقرارك وتفهم وضعك الضريبي. للتقديم الرسمي، لازم تستخدم موقع معتمد من الـ IRS أو تطبع وترسل بالبريد.

🚀
Quick Shortcut — اختصار سريع

Use push.bat to quickly save your project to Git. Just double-click it and it does git add . → commit → push automatically!

استخدم push.bat عشان تحفظ مشروعك على Git بسرعة. دبل كلك وهو يسوي كل شي تلقائي!
