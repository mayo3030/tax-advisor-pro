New York Tax Overview

نظرة عامة على ضرائب نيويورك

NYC Local Income Tax

New York City residents pay an additional local income tax with brackets ranging from 3.078% to 3.876% based on income. This is on top of state tax, not instead of it.

سكان نيويورك سيتي يدفعون ضريبة محلية إضافية فوق ضريبة الولاية. مش بدل الضريبة — إضافة عليها.

Chapter 2

NY Deductions & Credits

الخصومات والائتمانات في نيويورك

New York offers several state-specific tax credits that can significantly reduce your tax bill. These are in addition to federal credits.

Empire State Child Credit

👶 Empire State Child Credit

Up to $1,000 per child under age 4
Up to $330 per child ages 4–16
Income limits: $6,000 for single filers, $7,500 for head of household, $10,000 for married filing jointly.

ائتمان نيويورك للأطفال لحد ١,٠٠٠$ لكل طفل تحت ٤ سنين، و٣٣٠$ من ٤ لـ ١٦ سنة. فيها حد أقصى للدخل.

New York EITC

💰 NY Earned Income Tax Credit

New York's EITC is approximately 30% of your federal EITC. If you qualify for federal EITC, you automatically qualify for NY EITC (no additional application). Maximum credit varies by number of children.

ائتمان نيويورك هو حوالي ٣٠٪ من الائتمان الفيدرالي. لو اتأهلت للفيدرالي، اتأهلت للنيويورك تلقائي.

College Tuition Credit

🎓 Tuition Credit and Deduction

Credit: Up to $400 per year if your NY adjusted gross income is under $80,000 (single) or $160,000 (married).
Deduction: Instead of the credit, you can deduct up to $10,000 in tuition and fees if your income exceeds the credit limits.

رصيد التعليم لحد ٤٠٠$ أو خصم لحد ١٠,٠٠٠$. اختار اللي أفضل بوضعك.

Pension & Retirement Exemption

✅
Retiree Benefit

If you are age 59½ or older, you can exclude up to $20,000 of eligible retirement income from New York taxable income. Social Security is 100% exempt. Military retirement pay is fully exempt regardless of age.

لو عمرك ٥٩.٥ أو أكثر، ممكن تخصم لحد ٢٠,٠٠٠$ من دخل التقاعد. الضمان الاجتماعي معفوف ١٠٠٪. رتبة عسكرية معفوفة كاملة.

Child and Dependent Care Credit

New York allows a credit for childcare expenses up to certain limits. This is calculated on your NY tax return (Form IT-201) alongside the federal credit.

Chapter 3

New York Filing

كيفية التقديم في نيويورك

New York uses its own tax forms and has specific filing requirements depending on your residency status.

Forms You'll Need

📋 Form IT-201 (Resident Return)

Use this form if you are a New York resident (domiciled in NY or maintained a permanent home here). It's similar to the federal 1040 but specifically for New York taxes.

استخدم IT-201 لو تسكن في نيويورك. فيها فراغات للدخل والخصومات والائتمانات النيويوركية.

📋 Form IT-201-NR (Non-Resident Return)

Use this form if you earned income in New York but are not a NY resident. You only report the income earned in New York, not your total income from other states.

اذا ما تسكن في نيويورك بس تشتغل فيها، استخدم IT-201-NR وأبلّغ عن الدخل من نيويورك بس.

Key Deadlines

📅
2025 Tax Year Deadlines

Due Date: April 15, 2026
Extension (Form IT-370): October 15, 2026 (automatic 6-month extension)
Note: Extensions extend the filing deadline only, not the payment deadline. Pay any tax owed by April 15.

آخر موعد: ١٥ أبريل ٢٠٢٦. تمديد ممكن لـ ١٥ أكتوبر بس لازم تدفع بـ ١٥ أبريل.

Estimated Tax Payments

If you expect to owe $300 or more in New York income tax, you must pay quarterly estimated taxes. Use Form IT-2105 to calculate. Due dates are:

January 15 (for prior year), April 15, June 15, and September 15. If your 1099 income changes during the year, you can recalculate your estimated payments.

اذا متوقع تدفع ٣٠٠$ أو أكثر، ادفع تقديرات كل ٣ شهور. المواعيد: يناير، أبريل، يونيو، سبتمبر.

NYC & Local Taxes

ضرائب نيويورك سيتي والضرائب المحلية

New York City has one of the highest combined tax rates in the nation. Residents pay state, local, and potential metropolitan surcharges.

Combined Tax Impact

A NYC resident in the 10.9% state bracket plus 3.876% city bracket plus 0.375% MCTD surcharge faces an effective combined rate of 15.151%. This is among the highest in the U.S.

السكان في نيويورك سيتي يواجهون نسب ضريبية عالية جداً — الولاية + المدينة + اضافية = ١٥٪ أو أكثر!

MCTD Surcharge (Metropolitan Commuter Transportation Duty)

NYC residents and workers in the metro area are subject to a 0.375% MCTD surcharge on New York-source income over certain thresholds. This applies to both salaries and self-employment income.

Yonkers Earnings Tax

If you work or live in Yonkers, you pay an additional 1.25% to 1.75% earnings tax on top of state and local NYC taxes (if applicable).

Sales Tax

🛒 New York & NYC Sales Tax

State Sales Tax: 4%
Local Sales Tax (outside NYC): Up to 4.875%
NYC Combined Sales Tax: 8.875% (4% state + 4.5% city + 0.375% MCTD)
Note: Groceries and most prescription drugs are exempt.

ضريبة المبيعات في نيويورك ٤٪ ولايات + محلي. في نيويورك سيتي تصير ٨.٨٧٥٪. المواد الغذائية معفوفة.

Chapter 5

Self-Employment in NY

العمل الحر في نيويورك

If you have 1099 self-employment income in New York, you owe both federal and state self-employment taxes.

Federal Self-Employment Tax

All self-employed individuals pay a flat 15.3% federal self-employment tax (12.4% for Social Security + 2.9% for Medicare) on 92.35% of net self-employment income. There is no separate New York self-employment tax.

New York Income Tax on SE Income

Your net self-employment income is subject to New York state and local income tax at your applicable bracket (4% to 10.9% state, plus NYC rates if applicable).

💼
SE Income Example

If you have $50,000 in net SE income:

• Federal SE tax: $50,000 × 92.35% × 15.3% = $7,074
• NY state tax (6.85% bracket): $50,000 × 6.85% = $3,425
• NYC tax (3.5%): $50,000 × 3.5% = $1,750
• Total: $12,249 (24.5% effective rate)

لو عندك ٥٠,٠٠٠$ دخل حر، الضرائب الاتحادية + الولاية + المدينة تصير حوالي ١٢,٠٠٠$ (٢٤.٥٪).

MCTD Threshold

The 0.375% MCTD surcharge applies to net self-employment income exceeding $150,000 in New York City.

Estimated Payments Required

If you expect to owe $300 or more in combined federal and state taxes, make quarterly estimated payments via the IRS and New York (Form IT-2105).

Chapter 6

NY Tips & Pitfalls

نصائح وتحذيرات ضرائب نيويورك

The Highest Taxes in the Nation

⚠️
Reality Check

NYC residents pay among the highest combined income, payroll, and sales taxes in the United States. A resident earning $200,000 may face an effective combined federal + NY rate exceeding 45%.

نيويورك سيتي من أغلى الأماكن للضرائب في أمريكا. دخل ٢٠٠,٠٠٠$ قد يواجه نسبة مركبة ٤٥٪ أو أكثر!

Retiree Paradise (with Limits)

✅
Retirement Income Breaks

New York offers generous breaks for retirees age 59½+: up to $20,000 in retirement income exclusion, 100% Social Security exemption, and full military retirement exemption. This attracts retirees despite high working-age taxes.

نيويورك لطيفة مع التقاعدين — اعفاء ضريبي ٢٠,٠٠٠$ من التقاعد + ضمان اجتماعي كامل معفوف.

Standard Deduction vs. Federal

The New York standard deduction ($8,000 single / $16,050 MFJ) is lower than the federal deduction ($15,000 single / $30,000 MFJ). You must file a NY return even if you don't owe federal tax.

Property Tax Relief Proposals

New York periodically proposes property tax relief for homeowners and seniors, but these programs change frequently. Check tax.ny.gov for current programs and eligibility.

No Separate State SE Tax

💡 Good News for Freelancers

Unlike some states, New York does NOT have a separate state self-employment tax. You only pay the federal SE tax (15.3%) plus NY income tax on your net SE income. This saves self-employed individuals money compared to states with dual SE taxes.

نيويورك ما فيها ضريبة عمل حر منفصلة — بس الفيدرالي ١٥.٣٪ وضريبة الدخل. هاي أخبار حلوة للعاملين بالحر!

NYC vs. Suburbs: Tax Planning

Many high-income earners move just outside NYC to save on the 3.5% to 3.876% local tax. Living in Westchester, New Jersey, or Long Island can significantly reduce taxes despite commuting costs. However, New York considers "domicile" based on intent and connections, not just residence.

Nonresident Status

If you moved out of New York, you may still owe NY tax on income earned while resident or income sourced to NY. Use Form IT-201-NR to report only NY-source income. Keep records of your move date and documentation of new state residency.

🔑
Record Keeping

Keep all documentation: W-2s, 1099s, receipts for deductions, and proof of state/city residence changes. New York audits residency claims carefully, especially for high-income filers.

احفظ كل الأوراق والإيصالات. نيويورك تدقق على ادّعاءات السكن خاصة للأعلى دخلاً.
