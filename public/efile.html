🚀 E-Filing Options

⭐ Recommended

IRS Direct File

FREE

File directly with the IRS using their official free e-filing tool. No third-party software required.

  • File directly with the IRS
  • No software fees
  • Faster processing (8-14 days for refunds)
  • ⚠ Limited to ~25 states
Check if your state is supported at directfile.irs.gov

IRS Free File

FREE

Use partner software providers on irs.gov/freefile. Best if Direct File doesn't support your state.

  • Multiple partner software options
  • Covers all states
  • AGI under $84,000
  • User-friendly interfaces

Download & Mail

FREE

Generate a completed IRS Form 1040 PDF. Print and mail it to the IRS.

  • Complete form generation
  • Works everywhere
  • Keep physical records
  • ⚠ Slower processing (4-6 weeks)

📋 Step-by-Step Filing Guide

1

Check Your Eligibility

Visit directfile.irs.gov and verify your state is supported. Currently available in approximately 25 states. Check back throughout the season as more states are added.

2

Gather Required Documents

  • Prior year tax return (for AGI verification)
  • Your Social Security card
  • IP PIN (if you have one)
  • W-2 forms from all employers
  • 1099 forms (interest, dividends, 1099-NEC, etc.)
  • Supporting documentation (receipts, statements)
3

Transfer Your Data

Copy your tax summary (see Export section below) or manually enter your information into IRS Direct File. The platform guides you through each section of Form 1040.

4

Review & Sign

Carefully review all entries. Use your ID to authenticate with IRS systems. Sign and submit electronically.

5

Confirmation & Status

You'll receive a confirmation number. Save this for your records. Track your refund or payment at IRS.gov using your SSN and filing status.

1

Visit IRS Free File

Go to irs.gov/freefile and enter your income to verify eligibility (typically AGI under $84,000).

2

Choose a Partner Software

Review the list of participating software providers. All offer free filing for qualifying taxpayers. Choose based on your needs and complexity.

  • Intuit TurboTax Free
  • H&R Block Free
  • TaxAct Free
  • And 10+ other partners
3

Prepare Your Information

Gather the same documents as Direct File: W-2s, 1099s, prior year AGI, and any deduction documentation.

4

Enter & Review

The software guides you through each form. Use the exported tax summary from Tax Advisor Pro to speed up data entry. Verify all amounts carefully.

5

E-File & Track

Submit your return electronically. You'll receive an acceptance confirmation. Refunds typically process within 8-14 days with e-filing.

1

Generate Completed Form 1040

Use the "Download as PDF" button in the Export section below to generate your completed IRS Form 1040.

2

Print in Color

Print the PDF on plain white 8.5" x 11" paper in color (if possible). Use high-quality settings for best OCR readability at the IRS.

3

Sign & Date

Sign and date the form in blue or black ink. Both spouses must sign if filing jointly. Print your name and SSN clearly.

4

Attach Supporting Documents

  • Copy of W-2 forms (all)
  • Copy of 1099 forms (all)
  • Schedule C (if self-employed)
  • Schedule A (if itemizing)
  • Any other required schedules
5

Mail to the IRS

Mail your complete return to the IRS address for your state. Include a check if you owe tax (made payable to "United States Treasury"). Keep a copy for your records.

Processing typically takes 4-6 weeks for paper returns.
