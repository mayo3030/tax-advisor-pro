Chapter 1

NJ Tax Overview

نظرة عامة على ضرائب نيو جيرسي

New Jersey has a progressive income tax system with rates ranging from 1.4% to 10.75% across 7-8 tax brackets. Unlike the federal system, New Jersey does not have a standard deduction — instead, you claim personal exemptions and dependent exemptions to reduce your taxable income. نيو جيرسي فيها ضرائب تصاعدية من ١.٤٪ لحد ١٠.٧٥٪. بخلاف الفيدرالي، ما في خصم معياري — بدل ما تاخد خصم ثابت، بتطالب بإعفاءات شخصية وإعفاءات المعالين.

🏛️ The NJ Tax Difference

Every year you file a Form NJ-1040 (or NJ-1040NR for non-residents) to report your income, exemptions, deductions, and credits. Your state tax is calculated separately from your federal tax.

كل سنة بتعبي فورم NJ-1040 عشان تبلّغ عن دخلك والإعفاءات والخصومات. الضريبة الولائية تحتسب منفصلة عن الفيدرالي.

Important Difference

NJ rates are much lower than federal, and there's no standard deduction. This means you claim exemptions instead: $1,000 per person + $1,500 per dependent. If you don't have dependents, you still get a $1,000 exemption.

معدلات نيو جيرسي أقل بكثير من الفيدرالي. ما في خصم ثابت — بدل ما تاخد خصم معياري، بتطالب بإعفاءات: ١٠٠٠$ لكل شخص + ١٥٠٠$ لكل معال.

Filing Threshold (When You Must File)

Single: Your gross income must be at least $10,000. Married Filing Jointly: Combined income must be at least $20,000. If your income is below these thresholds, you may still want to file to claim refundable tax credits like NJ EITC. أعزب: الدخل لازم يكون ١٠ آلاف$ على الأقل. متزوج: الدخل المشترك لازم يكون ٢٠ ألف$ على الأقل.

✅
Even If Below Threshold

Even if your income is below the filing threshold, file anyway if you had taxes withheld or if you're eligible for EITC or other credits — you might get a refund!

حتى لو دخلك أقل من المتطلب، قدّم الإقرار لو انخصمت منك ضرائب أو لو أهل للـ EITC — ممكن ترجعلك فلوس!

Married Filing Separately in NJ

New Jersey allows married couples to file separately. Each spouse gets a $1,000 exemption plus $1,500 per dependent (split as they decide). This is less common than federal but useful in some situations.

نيو جيرسي تسمح للمتزوجين يقدموا إقرارات منفصلة. كل واحد ياخد ١٠٠٠$ إعفاء.

Chapter 2

NJ Deductions & Credits

الخصومات والائتمانات في نيو جيرسي

New Jersey offers several state-specific deductions and credits that can significantly reduce your tax bill. Combined with federal credits, these can make a huge difference.

🏠 Property Tax Deduction

Up to $15,000 of property taxes paid on your home are deductible from your NJ income. Renters can deduct 18% of rent (up to about $2,700). This is one of the biggest NJ deductions.

خصم الضريبة العقارية لحد ١٥٠٠٠$ من ضريبة البيت اللي بتدفعها. المستأجرين يخصموا ١٨٪ من الإيجار.

🎓 Tuition Deduction

Up to $10,000 per year for qualified college tuition and fees (at accredited institutions). This applies to you, your spouse, or dependents. The deduction is taken on Form NJ-1040, not federal Form 1040.

خصم الرسوم الجامعية لحد ١٠٠٠٠$ في السنة.

💰 NJ EITC (Earned Income Tax Credit)

New Jersey's EITC is 40% of the federal EITC you claim. If the federal EITC gives you $2,000, the NJ EITC adds another $800 — a total of $2,800. Fully refundable.

الـ EITC في نيو جيرسي هو ٤٠٪ من الـ EITC الفيدرالي. لو الفيدرالي أعطاك ٢٠٠٠$، نيو جيرسي تزيدك ٨٠٠$ — المجموع ٢٨٠٠$!

ANCHOR Property Tax Relief Program

If you own your home or are a renter with low income, you may qualify for the ANCHOR program (Affordable New Jersey Communities and Housing Relief for Homeowners).

ANCHOR Details

ANCHOR is a one-time payment sent directly to homeowners and renters. You don't deduct it — you just receive it. Check NJ tax form NJ-1040 or visit nj.gov/anchor to see if you qualify. Income limits are high ($250,000), so many NJ residents benefit.

الـ ANCHOR دفعة لمرة واحدة توصللك مباشرة. ما بتخصمها من الضريبة — يعني إضافة صافية على دخلك!

Stay NJ Program (For Seniors)

Homeowners age 50+ with low income can get 50% of property tax paid, up to $6,500 per year. Requires annual application but doesn't need to be renewed if your situation doesn't change.

برنامج "ابقى في نيو جيرسي" للمسنين — يخفض الضريبة العقارية بـ ٥٠٪ لحد ٦,٥٠٠$ كل سنة.

🏡 Homestead Property Tax Exemption

Primary residence homeowners may qualify for a $0 property tax or significant reduction on their primary home (each county has different programs). Check your county assessor's office.

بيوت السكن الأساسي قد تتمتع بإعفاء من ضريبة الملكية أو تخفيف كبير.

Other NJ Credits

Child and Dependent Care Credit: Up to 30% of childcare expenses (max $3,000 expenses = $900 credit). Earned Income Tax Credit (Federal): Also applies in NJ — don't forget to claim it on your state return too!

Chapter 3

NJ Filing Requirements

متطلبات التقديم في نيو جيرسي

New Jersey has its own filing deadline, forms, and rules. Make sure you understand both NJ and federal requirements if you're a NJ resident.

📋 Filing Forms

NJ-1040: Used by NJ residents. NJ-1040NR: Used by non-residents with NJ-source income (like rental property or business in NJ). NJ-1040-S: For pass-through entities and S corporations.

فورم NJ-1040 للمقيمين في نيو جيرسي. NJ-1040NR للغير مقيمين اللي عندهم دخل من نيو جيرسي.

Key Dates

Regular Deadline: April 15, 2026 (same as federal). Extension Deadline: October 15, 2026 (6-month extension). Important: Even if you file for an extension, you must pay 80% of your estimated tax by April 15 or face penalties and interest.

الموعد العادي: ١٥ أبريل ٢٠٢٦. التمديد: ١٥ أكتوبر ٢٠٢٦. لازم تدفع على الأقل ٨٠٪ من الضريبة المتوقعة بالموعد الأول حتى لو مددت.

⚠️
Extension ≠ Automatic Forgiveness

Filing an extension gives you time to prepare your return, but not time to pay taxes. If you owe money, pay by April 15 even if you file the extension. Interest starts accruing immediately after April 15.

التمديد ما معناته تأجيل الدفع! لازم تدفع ما تقدره بالموعد الأول، وإلا بتتفاقم الفوائس والغرامات.

Estimated Quarterly Tax Payments (NJ-1040-ES)

If you owe more than $400 in state taxes with no withholding, you must make quarterly estimated payments using Form NJ-1040-ES. Dates: April 15, June 15, September 15, and January 15.

📅
Quarterly Payment Rule

Calculate your expected annual tax, divide by 4, and pay by each quarterly date. Underpayment can trigger a penalty even if you pay in full by April 15 next year.

احسب الضريبة المتوقعة السنوية وقسمها على ٤، وادفع كل ثلاث شهور. عدم الدفع في الموعد المحدد ممكن يسبب غرامة حتى لو دفعت كل شي بعدين.

Where to File

New Jersey has no income tax on retirement income (pensions, Social Security, IRA withdrawals) for residents over 62. This is huge! Additionally, NJ does not tax earned income of residents over 62 in some cases — check eligibility.

نيو جيرسي ما تفرض ضريبة على دخل التقاعد (المعاشات والضمان الاجتماعي) للمسنين فوق ٦٢ سنة!

Chapter 4

Property Taxes in NJ

الضريبة العقارية في نيو جيرسي

New Jersey has the highest property tax burden in the nation — the average homeowner pays about 2.5% of property value annually. This is why NJ offers so many relief programs.

نيو جيرسي عندها أعلى ضرائب عقارية في أمريكا! لذلك فيه برامج كثير عشان تساعد السكان.

⚠️
Reality Check

A $300,000 home in NJ typically pays $7,500-9,000 annually in property taxes. Combined with federal deductions, state programs, and credits, you can offset a significant portion.

بيت بـ ٣٠٠ ألف دولار في نيو جيرسي يدفع حوالي ٧,٥٠٠ لـ ٩,٠٠٠$ سنة ضرائب عقارية!

How to Maximize Property Tax Relief

1

Deduct on Federal Taxes

Up to $10,000 in state and local property taxes (SALT) can be deducted on your federal Form 1040. If your NJ property taxes exceed this, the excess is not deductible.

2

Deduct on NJ Taxes

Up to $15,000 in NJ property taxes deduct from NJ taxable income (no federal SALT limit applied here). Renters deduct 18% of rent instead.

3

Apply for ANCHOR

Annual program for homeowners/renters with income under $250,000. Provides $1,000-1,500 per year. No deduction — it's direct relief.

4

Check County/Local Programs

Your county assessor's office may offer homestead exemptions, senior exemptions, or other local relief. Most are automatic or require simple annual application.

Assessment Challenges

If you believe your home's assessed value is too high, file an assessment challenge with your county assessor by April 1 each year. If successful, your property tax can be reduced retroactively.

لو تعتقد التقييم العقاري غالي، تقدر تطعن فيه قبل ١ أبريل. لو نجحت، الضريبة تنخفض.

💡
Pro Tip: Sales Ratio

If properties in your neighborhood recently sold for less than their assessed value, use this in your challenge. Assessors must assess at "true market value," and recent sales prove what that is.

لو بيوت في حيك اتباعت برخص من التقييم، استخدم هاي المعلومة بالطعن.

Pass-Through Entity Tax (BAIT) as SALT Workaround

If you own a pass-through entity (LLC, S-Corp, partnership) and pay high NJ taxes, you can elect to pay Pass-Through Entity Tax (BAIT) at 10.9% on income over $1 million. This shift reduces your federal taxable income and helps you avoid the $10,000 SALT cap.

لو عندك بزنس كـ LLC أو S-Corp بدخل عالي، تقدر تختار تدفع ضريبة المؤسسة المالكة (BAIT) بدل الضريبة الشخصية — هاي تساعد تتجنب حد الـ SALT الـ ١٠ آلاف$.

Chapter 5

Self-Employment in NJ

العمل الحر والضرائب في نيو جيرسي

Self-employed individuals (1099 contractors, freelancers, business owners) owe both federal self-employment tax (15.3%) and NJ income tax on net profits. NJ treats self-employment income differently than federal.

💼 Federal Self-Employment Tax

You pay 15.3% on 92.35% of net self-employment income: 12.4% for Social Security (capped at ~$168,000 income) and 2.9% for Medicare (no cap). You can deduct half of this tax.

تدفع ١٥.٣٪ على دخل العمل الحر — ١٢.٤٪ للضمان الاجتماعي و٢.٩٪ لـ Medicare. بتقدر تخصم نصها من الضريبة.

🏛️ NJ Income Tax on Self-Employment

NJ taxes self-employment income as "Net Profits From Business" on Schedule C (federal) transferred to NJ Form NJ-1040. You pay NJ's progressive rates (1.4%-10.75%) on your net profit after deductions.

نيو جيرسي تحسب ضرائبها على الدخل الصافي من البزنس بعد الخصومات.

Business Expense Deductions

Before calculating NJ tax, deduct all legitimate business expenses (same as federal):

💼
Deductible Business Expenses

Home Office: $5/sq ft up to 300 sq ft ($1,500 max) or actual expenses method. Vehicle: 70¢/mile in 2025 or actual expenses (gas, repairs, insurance). Supplies: Computer, software, tools, printer. Phone/Internet: Business portion of monthly bill. Professional Services: Accountant, lawyer, bookkeeper fees. Meals & Entertainment: 50% of business meals. Travel: Hotels, flights, car rentals for business trips.

مكتب في البيت، سيارة، أدوات، هاتف وإنترنت، خدمات المحاسب، وجبات عمل، السفر — كلها تنخصم.

NJ Quarterly Estimated Taxes

If you expect to owe more than $400 in NJ state tax for the year, you must make quarterly estimated payments via NJ Form NJ-1040-ES. Dates: April 15, June 15, September 15, January 15.

⚠️
Underpayment Penalty

Failing to pay quarterly can trigger an "underpayment penalty" even if you pay 100% of your tax by the extension deadline (October 15). The penalty is based on how much you should have paid each quarter.

عدم دفع الأقساط التقديرية ممكن يسبب غرامة حتى لو دفعت المبلغ كاملا بعدين.

  • Workaround for the $10,000 federal SALT cap
  • Potential savings of 2-3% on high incomes

توفير على الضرائب الشخصية، تجنب حد الـ SALT، وتوفير إضافي على الدخل العالي.

Chapter 6

NJ Tips & Common Pitfalls

نصائح وأخطاء شائعة في نيو جيرسي
❌ Pitfall #1: Forgetting No Standard Deduction

The biggest mistake: expecting a standard deduction like federal. NJ doesn't have one. Claim your $1,000 personal exemption + $1,500 per dependent instead.

أكبر غلط: توقع خصم معياري مثل الفيدرالي. نيو جيرسي ما عندها. بدل ما تخصم ١٥ ألف$ معياري، بتطالب بـ ١٠٠٠$ إعفاء شخصي + ١٥٠٠$ لكل معال.

❌ Pitfall #2: Ignoring Property Tax Deductions

Up to $15,000 of NJ property taxes are deductible. Don't miss this! Renters: don't forget 18% of rent deduction (up to ~$2,700).

خصم ضريبة البيت لحد ١٥٠٠٠$ واحد من أكبر الخصومات في نيو جيرسي. ما تنساه!

❌ Pitfall #3: Missing ANCHOR Payments

ANCHOR is automatic for most homeowners and renters under $250,000 income, but you have to be enrolled or check annually. Many NJ residents leave $1,000+ on the table by ignoring this.

برنامج ANCHOR معظم الناس يهملونه — تفقد ١٠٠٠$ لـ ١٥٠٠$ مجاني!

❌ Pitfall #4: Not Claiming NJ EITC

If you qualify for federal EITC, automatically claim NJ EITC too (40% of federal). It's refundable — you can get a refund even if you owe $0 in taxes.

الـ EITC في نيو جيرسي يعطيك ٤٠٪ إضافية على الـ EITC الفيدرالي!

Sales Tax & NJ Consumption Tax

NJ sales tax is a flat 6.625% statewide with no additional local taxes. Some items are exempt (groceries, clothing under $110, medications). While this isn't deductible on your tax return, it's important to know.

ضريبة المبيعات في نيو جيرسي ٦.٦٢٥٪ موحدة في كل الولاية. ما في ضرائب إضافية محلية.

✅
How to Maximize Tax Relief

1. Claim $1,000 personal exemption + $1,500 per dependent. 2. Deduct property taxes (up to $15,000) or 18% rent. 3. Claim NJ EITC (40% of federal). 4. Apply for ANCHOR ($1,000-1,500). 5. Check state tuition deduction ($10,000). 6. For high earners: consider BAIT if entity income > $1M. Combining all these can save $2,000-8,000+ depending on your situation.

اجمع بين الإعفاءات والخصومات والائتمانات والبرامج الخاصة لتوفر أقصى حد. ممكن توفر ٢٠٠٠ لـ ٨٠٠٠$ أو أكثر!

EITC Below Filing Threshold

Important: Even if your income is below $10,000 (single) or $20,000 (married), you should still file NJ taxes if you have EITC or had taxes withheld. Both are refundable and you'll get money back.

حتى لو دخلك أقل من المتطلب، قدّم إقرار لو عندك EITC أو انخصمت منك ضرائب — بترجعلك فلوس!

📞 Getting Help

NJ Division of Taxation: 609-588-2200 or nj.gov/taxation. Free VITA Programs: Many NJ nonprofits offer free tax preparation for low-income residents. Search "VITA near me" or visit irs.gov/vita.

لو احتجت مساعدة: خط الضرائب في نيو جيرسي أو مواقع تحضير الضرائب المجاني للدخل المنخفض.
